# AI Video Platform Style Selection Analysis

## Research Date: January 29, 2026

## Key Question: Do platforms offer explicit style selection (Cartoon, Realistic, Anime, News-like, Animated, Chalkboard, etc.) or just templates?

---

## FINDINGS BY PLATFORM

### 1. HeyGen (Avatar Platform)
- **Style Selection:** NO explicit visual style selector
- **Approach:** Template-based with USE-CASE categories (Marketing, Training, Sales, etc.)
- **Avatar Categories:** Persona-based (Professional, Casual, etc.) not visual style
- **Screenshot:** heygen_01_templates.webp, heygen_02_avatars.webp

### 2. Synthesia (Avatar Platform)
- **Style Selection:** LIMITED - Has "Modern", "Minimalistic", "Colorful" tags

- **Screenshot:** synthesia_01_template_categories.webp, synthesia_02_all_categories.webp

### 3. Runway (Generative Video)
- **Style Selection:** NO explicit visual style selector
- **Approach:** Prompt-driven with MOTION presets (Camera, Movement, Action)
- **Presets:** Focus on camera movements, not visual styles
- **Screenshot:** runway_01_main_interface.webp, runway_02_presets_camera.webp

### 5. Kling AI (Generative Video)

## SUMMARY: STYLE SELECTION APPROACHES

### Platforms WITH Explicit Visual Style Selection:
1. **Hailuo AI** - Style category tabs (ReStyle, Horror, etc.) with visual templates
2. **Pixverse** - Style category tabs (Stylistic Art, Anime & Playverse, etc.)
3. **Pika** - Pikaffects with some style transformations (90s Me, Anime Cat, Lo-Fi Me)

### Platforms WITH Use-Case Categories (Not Visual Styles):
1. **HeyGen** - Marketing, Training, Sales templates
2. **Synthesia** - Training, Onboarding, Corporate templates (some style tags like Modern, Minimalistic)
3. **Luma** - Film, Anime, Advertising, Gaming (use-case not visual style)
4. **Vidu** - Anime, Advertising, Social Media, Film (use-case categories)

### Platforms WITHOUT Style Selection (Prompt-Driven Only):
1. **Runway** - Motion presets only, no visual style selection
2. **Kling AI** - Pure prompt-driven, no style categories

---

## KEY INSIGHT

**Most platforms do NOT offer explicit visual style selection like "Cartoon vs Realistic vs News-like vs Chalkboard".**

Instead, they use:
1. **Template-based approach** - Pre-made templates organized by use-case
2. **Prompt-driven approach** - Users describe style in text prompts
3. **Reference-based approach** - Upload images to transfer style (Luma, Vidu)
4. **Effect-based approach** - Apply transformations/filters (Pika Pikaffects)

**Only Hailuo AI and Pixverse** have clear visual style category systems with templates organized by aesthetic style (not just use-case).
